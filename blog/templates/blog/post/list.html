The Smart Energy Research Group

The Research group consist of group of persons whose aim is to build smart meters
and also write Webapplications that allow the smart devices communicate with the
users it functionality, status and total energy used

THE RESEARCH PROJECT HAS BEEN DIVIDED INTO MODULES SUCH AS:

Research On Smart Meters

This includes documentations on smart meters, types and off grid capacity.

Web Development and Hardware Team

The web development teams build a responsive webpage for the smart devices
and the hardware team oversees the building,testing and placing of the smart meters

The integration and Deployement Team

These team are in charge of deploying smart meter getting reports on them to the web platform regularly and giving feedback to other teams.
